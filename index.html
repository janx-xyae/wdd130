Michoacan

Michoacan, Mexico is a state known for its rich culture, history, and natural beauty. It is famous for its beautiful landscapes, traditions, handicrafts, and delicious food. Some of its most recognized places include Lake Patzcuaro, the Monarch Butterfly Reserve, and the traditional Day of the Dead celebrations. Michoacan preserves many Indigenous customs and traditions that are an important part of Mexican identity. It is a unique place where history, nature, and culture come together.
